About Us

WashGo was created to solve the inconvenience of traditional laundries in Moroccan cities. We noticed that busy professionals, students, and families were spending precious hours on laundry every week.

Built specifically for Moroccan cities, our service connects you with trusted local laundries through our convenient pickup and delivery system. We're focused on convenience, fairness, and reliability - making your life easier while supporting local businesses.
